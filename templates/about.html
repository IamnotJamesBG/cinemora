About Us

Our Mission

FG_anime is dedicated to providing anime fans with a seamless streaming experience. We bring the latest and greatest anime directly to you, using reliable third-party APIs.

What We Do

We do not store any content on our servers. Instead, we stream anime episodes using trusted third-party sources, ensuring that you have access to a wide range of anime series.

Our Vision

We strive to be the go-to platform for anime enthusiasts by offering high-quality streaming and a user-friendly interface.

Open Source

FG_anime is an open-source project. We believe in the power of community collaboration. You can view, contribute, and support the development of FG_anime on our GitHub repository.
